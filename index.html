Social Distancing Sensor

Social Distancing Sensor

A system consisting of devices that communicate with each other through both ultrasonic signals and radio frequency signals.

The devices are programmed with the use of microcontrollers so that when any two units come within six feet of each other, both units begin beeping to alert the users.

Embedded C | Microcontrollers | Analog Hardware

Automatic Salt Spreader

EZ Melter: Automatic Salt Spreader

An automatic, rotating sprinkler device that emits salt onto non-drivable surfaces such as ramps and staircases to melt snow and ice.

Contains a servo motor to rotate the device and ensure that the salt is spread at a wide angle, a DC motor to control the spinning of the wheel, and a linear actuator to open/close a small door and regulate the flow of salt.

Arduino | Hardware

Electronic Combination Lock

Electronic Combination Lock

A lock, created with an FPGA board and programmed using VHDL, that compares a user’s 4-digit keypad input to a hardwired passcode.

Contains features such as LED indicators and a temporary lock-out mode that engages after three consecutive failed input attempts.

Tiny Search Engine

Tiny Search Engine

A small search engine that consists of three subsystems:
1) a crawler that crawls the web and retrieves webpages starting from a “seed” URL
2) an indexer that reads the document files produced by the crawler and builds an index of the number of occurrences of a word on a particular webpage
3) a querier that answers search queries and returns a list of webpages ranked by relevance.
